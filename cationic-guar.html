Cationic Guar
(Guar Hydroxypropyltrimonium Chloride)

APPLICATIONS

Cationic guar is a versatile ingredient that enhances texture, conditioning, and stability across hair, skin, and body care formulations. Its natural film-forming and moisturizing properties make it a preferred choice for premium personal care products.

Future Outlook

With rising demand for natural and sustainable ingredients, cationic guar is becoming a must-have in hair and skin care innovations. It delivers luxury textures, enhanced performance, and eco-friendly formulation benefits.
