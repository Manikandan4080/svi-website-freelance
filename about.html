About us

We introduce ourselves as one of the largest Manufacturers and Exporters of Guar Gum Powder and its derivatives for more than 12 years. We are an ISO 9001: 2008 and HACCP (ISO 22000: 2005) Certified Company. Our plant is fully automatic with latest technology, quality control laboratory and trained skilled personnel in our plant. We are exporting this product to more than 45 countries worldwide with an annual production of over 15000 MT.

We cater to a wide range of industries such as Food, Oil/Gas Drilling, Fracking, Textile, Paper, Explosives, Constructions, Paints, Mining, etc. We are supplying our products to all over the world. Our customers are Multinational, world-renowned companies. Our Commitment towards our quality and the customer has brought SHREE VIJAYLAXMI ENTERPRISES worldwide recognition of a high quality guar gum supplier.
